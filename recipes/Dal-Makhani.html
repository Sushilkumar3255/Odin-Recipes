Dal-Makhani Recipe

Description

This quick lunch or dinner pizza is made with prepared puff pastry dough as a shortcut. The results are a light, flaky crust for a unique pizza. Feel free to modify with your favorite pizza toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole black lentils (urad dal)
  • 1/4 cup red kidney beans (rajma)
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Coriander leaves (cilantro) for garnishing

Steps

  1. Rinse the urad dal and rajma in water and soak them in enough water overnight or for at least 8 hours
  2. Drain the soaked lentils and beans and transfer them to a pressure cooker. Add enough water and a pinch of salt. Pressure cook on medium heat for about 20-25 minutes or until they are tender
  3. In a pan, heat ghee over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté for 2-3 minutes until they turn golden brown
  4. Add minced garlic and grated ginger and sauté for another minute
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and sauté until they become soft and mushy
  6. Add garam masala, red chili powder, and turmeric powder. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes
  7. Add the cooked lentils and beans along with the water to the pan. Mix well and cook for 5-10 minutes
  8. Mash some of the lentils and beans with the back of a spoon to thicken the gravy
  9. Add cream and salt to taste. Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes
  10. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan bread
